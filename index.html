Warm Ups

  • Jog around courts
  • Glute kicks
  • Lunges (add twist if balance is good enough)
  • Open and close the gate
  • Shoulder circles
  • High kicks
  • Shuffling

Stretches

  • Touch toes
  • Shoulder rotations
  • Calf stretch
  • Forearm stretch
  • Quad stretch

Fitness

  • Ladder drills
  • Line sprints
  • Frog Jumps
  • Lunges
  • Toe touches
  • Line jumps
  • M&M

Practice

  • Drop feed balls
  • Instruct to try and hit crosscourt/down the line
  • 4 ball drills
  • V-drills
  • Vertical movement drills
  • Hit ball and go through the ladder
  • Hit x balls in to stop

Teach/Reinforce Strokes

  • Show the motion
  • Ask kids to go through the motions
  • Point out any major flaws in stroke patterns

Games

  • Balancing ball on the racquet
  • Rolling ball across the court
  • See how many times you can bounce ball on racquet
  • Mini-tennis
  • Around the world mini-tennis
